Lin-Manuel Miranda

Biography

Lin-Manuel Miranda, also known as the musical genius behind Hamilton was born on 16th of January to Luis A. Miranda Jr and Luz Towns-Miranda with little sister Luz Miranda-Crespo following close behind. Lin has a pretty big repertoire of awards for his multiple contributions to other films and musical productions, such as, but not limited to

  • a Gold Record in Brasil for "We Know The Way"
  • a Gold Record in the US for the album "In The Heights"
  • a Platinum Record in the UK for "We Know The Way"
and 9 Silver Records too! Overall he is pretty successful as both an actor and music producer. Generally it is known that when you want good film music you either ask Hans Zimmer or Lin-Manuel Miranda :)
