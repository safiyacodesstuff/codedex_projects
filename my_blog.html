About

Hi!
My name is Safiya and being a bored college sudent I find myself spending a lot of time on Netflix,
watching everything it has to offer. However, I do know that I should spend my time focussing on uni
so this blog serves as an outlet for me to ask myself the question:

Was the time I spent not studying worth it to watch this film/series?

I will rate the films and series I watched based on the folllowing criteria:
  • Originality: Is the story fresh and unique, or does it feel derivative?
  • Coherence: Does the plot make sense, and are there logical inconsistencies?
  • Pacing: Is the story well-paced, or does it drag in places?
  • Genre: Does it deliver what was promised?
  • Re-watchability: Is it something I'd watch again, and does it hold up on subsequent viewings?
Disclaimer:
I think you've already guessed it, but I will only rank films and series that are available on Netflix.
My rating here is highly subjective of course as it is my personal blog. Feel free to disagree ^^'
The films and series will be arranged alphabetically and the little number above them is just for aesthetics :]
Also, I really like comedic stuff, so most of these films and series will be (at least partially) under the Comedy tag.

Reviews

--- Films ---

1

Interstellar


  • Originality: It's awesome! A very new, very scientific and fascinating concept that I had never seen before.
  • Coherence: The story is consistent throughout and has a sort of looping end that ties the beginning and finale of the movie together.
  • Pacing: Although it's pretty time consuming being almost 3h long, I feel like that it's needed to explain the science behind it.
  • Genre: Science-Fiction -- It is exactly what I had expected from a film within that genre.
  • Re-watchability: Yes, it's one of my favourite films that I've watched countless times already and I wold recommend this film to anyone who's interested in all things space and science related.

Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐


2

Mean Girls


  • Originality: What is it if not THE classic teen girl movie. Everyone's seen it, everyone loves it, nothing compares.
  • Coherence: Yes, it is coherent.
  • Pacing: Well, there's not a lot of plot, but it's still iconic.
  • Genre: Dramedy -- It definetly fits.
  • Re-watchability: YES!!








Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐


3

Ocean's 8


  • Originality: I'd say it's not that original since it's basically just the female pendant to the Ocean's 11, 12, 13 etc. films with George Clooney.
  • Coherence: It is coherent and I like that the regisseur played with the flashbacks to enrich the story as it moves along.
  • Pacing: It's good, not too short and not too long. I liked it.
  • Genre: Heist-Comedy -- Yes, that describes it really well.
  • Re-watchability: I mean, it's good and the plot is really light, so it's pleasant to watch. I've rewatched it multiple times already but mostly as backround noise for other tasks.

Pitch Perfect 2

  • Coherence: Also very coherent. No plot holes in sight. (There isn't a lot of plot to put holes in anyway ^^')
  • Pacing: Good pacing. I approve.
  • Genre: Musical Comedy -- Yep, it's exactly that and I love it.
  • Re-watchability: Yes, definetly! It has Ohrwurmpotential (if you're not German, I'm sorry for you hahahaha).

5

The Intouchables


  • Originality: While there are certainly a lot of films about friendship, nothing I've watched ever came close to this.
  • Coherence: It's great! There's nothing else to say about that.
  • Pacing: It's a well paced, heartwarming story!
  • Genre: Dramedy -- It's more heartwarming than dramatic, but the comedy tag fits definetly.
  • Re-watchability: Yes, of course! I would recommend this film to everyone! Can't tell you how many times I've watched this movie already. Honestly, this is one of my alltime favourite films, that I keep coming back to, everytime I need some comfort.


Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐


6

Under Paris


  • Originality: It feels like every other shark movie, the only difference being that it's based in Paris, which varies from the classic beach shark attack cliche.
  • Coherence: While it has no plot holes, it's just weird. The protagonists act in ways no rationally thinking human being would ever act, but that just seems to be a thing in horror films, right?
  • Pacing: I guess the pacing's fine.
  • Genre: Horror -- No, it's not. As an average horror movie enjoyer I can definetly say that there is nothing horrific about this film.
  • Re-watchability: I mean, if you wanna have a good laugh at the absolute stupidity of the protagonists, go watch this one. You'll have a blast.

Overall Rating:
⭐⭐


--- Series ---

1

Brooklyn Nine-Nine


  • Originality: It's a cop series and I'm sure there's more of them, but this one's surely the best. It has to be, Jake said so hahahaha
  • Coherence: Every Episode is a different day in the lives of the detectives and therefore they're all connected and follow a coherent timeline.
  • Pacing: The story progresses over 8 seasons and it never gets boring. Mostly because it's filled with great humor.
  • Genre: Comedy/Sitcom/Action/Crime -- Perfect description, especially the comedy part. I swear it's extremly funny. Go watch it if you haven't yet!
  • Re-watchability: Well I watch it all the time, and by that I mean ALL the time. It's a constant in my life. Mostly as background noise. It's great! It's really great!

Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Re-watchability: Oh yes definetly! I have watched this endless times already! And I would recommend it to everyone who'd like a good laugh!





Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

5

Never Have I Ever


  • Originality: It's your classic coming-of-age series, so nothing special, aside from maybe the fact that the protagonist is Indian.
  • Coherence: Every episode is another Day in Devi's life. So yes, it's very coherent.
  • Pacing: Over 4 seasons we see Devi grow up and quite sympathetically so. The series is filled with a lot of humor and drama, which makes it very interesting up until the very last episode.
  • Genre: Dramedy/Lovestory -- Very acurate, especially because Devi's is always in complicated love triangles.
  • Re-watchability: I definetly have watched this multiple times already.

Overall Rating:
⭐⭐⭐⭐


6

The Umbrella Academy


  • Originality: Well, there are numerous series about super heroes, but never ones, where all of the heroes are siblings and their family is highly dysfunctional.
  • Coherence: It's been quite a while since I last watched this so I don't remember all the details, but I do remember that the story is coherent.
  • Pacing: The story progresses over 3, soon to be 4 seasons and is full of action, fun and drama. Safe to say, it never gets old.
  • Genre: Black Dramedy about Superheroes -- What can I say, it lives up to what it promises.
  • Re-watchability: I haven't yet but I definetly should rewatch this again before season 4 comes out.


Overall Rating:
