Lin-Manuel Miranda

Biography

Lin-Manuel Miranda, also known as the musical genius behind Hamilton was born on the
16th of January to father Luis A. Miranda Jr, mother Luz Towns-Miranda and older sister Luz Miranda-Crespo.

Lin has a pretty big repertoire of awards for his multiple contributions to other films and musical productions,
such as, but not limited to

  • a Gold Record in Brasil for "We Know The Way"
  • a Gold Record in the US for the album "In The Heights"
  • a Platinum Record in the UK for "We Know The Way"
and 9 Silver Records too!
Overall he is pretty successful as both an actor and music producer. Generally it is known that
if you want excellent film music you either ask Hans Zimmer or Lin-Manuel Miranda :)
